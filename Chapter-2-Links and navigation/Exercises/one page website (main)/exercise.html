Plot

The Dark Knight, directed by Christopher Nolan, is a superhero film that follows Batman as he faces off against the chaotic criminal mastermind known as the Joker.

Key Information

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Date: July 18, 2008

Genre: Superhero, Action, Crime

IMDb Rating: 9.0/10

Cast

Christian Bale as Batman / Bruce Wayne

Heath Ledger as The Joker

Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face

Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth

Gary Oldman as James Gordon

Characters

Batman / Bruce Wayne: The vigilante superhero of Gotham City, seeking to rid the city of crime.

The Joker: A psychopathic criminal mastermind who aims to create chaos in Gotham City.

Harvey Dent / Two-Face: Former District Attorney of Gotham City, driven to madness and seeking revenge.

Alfred Pennyworth: Bruce Wayne's loyal butler and confidant.

James Gordon: Commissioner of the Gotham City Police Department, an ally of Batman.
